NFIX ("Company", "we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use the Potato mobile application ("Service").

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

**Required for Registration** (via Google or Apple OAuth):
- Email address
- Display name or username
- Profile image URL (if provided)
- Unique identifier (Google ID or Apple ID)

**Optional Information** (provided by you):
- Investment profile (investor type, risk tolerance, experience, age range, primary market, monthly investment capacity)
- Portfolio information (tickers, share quantities, average purchase prices)

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

- Device information (OS type, OS version, device model)
- App version
- IP address
- Service usage records (access times, features used, error logs)
- AI Debate conversation content and metadata

### 1.3 Payment Information

- Payments are processed via Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do **not** directly collect credit card numbers, bank account information, or similar sensitive payment details.
- We receive the following from each store: subscription product, payment date, subscription status, transaction identifier.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

1. **Account Management**: Verify registration, authenticate users, prevent fraud and unauthorized access
2. **Service Provision**: Provide AI Debate functionality, deliver personalized information based on investment profile, store and recall conversation history
3. **Paid Service Delivery**: Manage subscriptions, verify payments, enforce usage limits
4. **Customer Support**: Process inquiries, resolve disputes, send notices
5. **Service Improvement**: Statistical analysis, develop new features, analyze usage patterns (in anonymized form)
6. **Marketing** (with separate consent): New feature announcements, event information
7. **Legal Compliance**: Fulfill obligations under applicable laws

## 9. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information:

1. **Administrative**: Internal access policies, regular staff training
2. **Technical**: 
   - All communications encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
   - Database access controlled via Row Level Security (RLS)
   - No storage of plaintext passwords (OAuth-only authentication)
   - Intrusion detection and prevention systems
3. **Physical**: Access controls at data centers (managed by service providers)

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 10. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The Service is a mobile app and does not use traditional web cookies. However, we may use:
- Device identifiers (IDFA, AAID) for analytics and personalization
- Local app storage for preferences

You can opt out of advertising identifiers via:
- **iOS**: Settings -> Privacy & Security -> Tracking -> Turn off "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
- **Android**: Settings -> Google -> Ads -> "Delete advertising ID"
